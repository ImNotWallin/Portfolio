Operational Meteorologist

Six years of full spectrum operational meteorology in the USAF. I have forecasted for a multitude of events, including everything from fun-runs to large-scale operations spanning 80,000 square miles while syncing multiple joint forecasters across 3 continetns. Operational meteorologists aren't your ordinary weathermen. We know how to exploit weather data to give you the high ground against your competitors and mother nature, no matter what realm your business operates in.
