001 : Operational Meteorology

I served six years in the USAF. I spent the first two years in the special operations weather pipeline and finished my last 4 years as a Staff Weather Officer at Fort Bragg. I have been trained and certified in SERE, Water Survival, Underwater Egress, Airborne Operations, and several other tactical areas. My background in the military gives me a competitive edge when it comes to forecasting, observing, terrain analysis, and providing environmental intelligence. After working with various operations at multiple levels, I've learned how to custom-tailor meteorological data to help any consumers needs.
