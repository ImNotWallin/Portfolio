004 : Awards & Certs

Here you will find some awards I received as a meteorologist as well as certifications from some of my Tech classes.

Awards

  • Awarded Air Force Commendation Medal for expertise in syncing 21 forecasters in 3 locations, guaranteeing the execution of transatlantic Joint Forcible Entry airborne operations for 2,000 paratroopers from 10 North Atlantic Treaty Organization Nations.
  • Awarded Army Commendation Medal as lead aviation forecaster and medical evacuation briefer. Hand-crafted 2,400 weather products for 474 Army sorties and enabled 3,500 flight hours over a six month deployment.
  • Awarded Air Force Achievement Medal for leading a three-person meteorological team during Hurricane Florence. Crafted 300 weather briefs, covering an 87,000 square mile operating area for 70k military members and civilians, Fort Bragg Generals, Army Chiefs of Staff, Pentagon, and the Presidential weather support team for Air Force One.
  • Awarded Army Achievement Medal for injecting meteorological data into a custom-tailored product, which provided critical daily weather reports for preserving operational readiness and mitigating weather impacts on the packing and movement of $300 million worth of equipment from Al Asad, Iraq to Bagram Airfield, Afghanistan.
